Belizean Chicken Stew

Description

Stewed chicken is one of the most popular dishes in Belize. Chicken pieces are stewed until the meat falls off the bone.

It's a very healthy dish but the limes and hot sauce make it very flavorful. Can be served with anything from rice, to tortillas, or even johnny cakes.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2lbs chicken
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 2 limes juiced
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil over medium heat
  2. Cook chicken in the hot oil until browned on the outside, about 10 minutes per side
  3. Mix onions, green bell pepper, tomato sauce, lime juice, garlic, salt and black pepper into chicken
  4. Let it simmer, adding water as needed, until stew is thickened, about 2 hours
